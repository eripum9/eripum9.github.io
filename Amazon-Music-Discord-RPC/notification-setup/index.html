Enable Notifications

Notification Enrichment reads track info from Amazon Music's Windows notifications. For this to work, notifications must be enabled in the Amazon Music app.

Note: Amazon Music only sends notifications when the app is minimized or not in focus. Keep it minimized to the taskbar for best results.
1

Open Settings

In the Amazon Music app, click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select Settings.

2

Enable Notifications

Scroll down until you see "Show notifications for track playing" and make sure the toggle is enabled.

✅ That's it! Notification Enrichment will now pick up full track metadata automatically.
