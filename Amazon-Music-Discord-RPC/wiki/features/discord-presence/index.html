Feature

Discord Presence

This is the main feature: Amazon Music playback appears on your Discord profile like a native music status.

What it does

Shows song title, artist, album artwork, progress timing, pause state, and an optional listen button through Discord Rich Presence.

When to use it

Use it whenever you want friends to see what you are playing in Amazon Music without switching music services.

How to configure it

Open Settings from the tray. Keep Rich Presence enabled, choose the listen button source, and configure privacy rules if there are tracks you do not want shown.

What to check if it does not work

  • Discord desktop must be running.
  • Amazon Music RPC must be running in the tray.
  • Private session must be off.
  • Diagnostics should show RPC and Discord as connected.

Privacy notes

Discord receives the presence fields needed to show your status. Use private session or keyword filters to hide playback.
