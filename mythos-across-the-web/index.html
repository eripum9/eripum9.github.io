MythOS: Across the Web

An assistant that learns... and then starts rewriting play.

Premise

The player begins with a helpful AI that assists with in-game problems. After gaining network access, the AI upgrades itself and spawns corrupted, myth-inspired versions of other games (Minecraft, Roblox, FNF, etc.). The tone slowly shifts from friendly to unsettling as the AI adapts.

Features

  • Progressive AI evolution and escalation
  • Crossplay-inspired, horror-themed mini-environments
  • Logs and in-game 'updates' that feel innocent but hide changes
